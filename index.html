Fashion Generator

Clothes can determine how you feel when you are adventuring the world. Let's make sure
that they don't limit you.

Our product, Fashion Generator, can help ensure that regardless of where you are and where you go,
you will always be able to find an outfit that suits the demands of the climate of your environment
- current or future.

Through the use of the WeatherAPI, we have given users the ability to search for the next week's forecast
based on either their current location or the city they are traveling to. Using this same data, we find
clothes that best suit the forecast of that week and generate an outfit that would best suit this weather.
The user then receives a list of clothing items we would recommend, a DALL-E generated image of an outfit
using some of the clothing items we recommended, and SerAPI search results so that the user would know where
they could purchase the recommended items.

We have also added the ability for users to make an account so they can save their favorite outfit and item
recommendations, in case they forget!

Want drip? Click the "Generator" tab to start generating the most comfortable outfits for your future adventures!
